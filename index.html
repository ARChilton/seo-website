Why do some websites get more visitors than others?

Not every website is created equal but all have a message to send. If you believe in your idea, your message, your product enough to want help spreading that message then you are in the right place.

Ideas excite us, nothing is better than the excitement of hearing a new idea and we love getting on-board to help them spread, grow and live.

How do we help your ideas grow?

We are a search engine optimisation (SEO) business and we analyse and improve your websites to help you to drive greater web traffic to your website. We do this by undertaking the following 5 simple steps to your web success:

Step 1.Tell us who you are.

Before we start any project it is important to us to get an understanding of why you and your business does what it does. What is your reason for existence?

Why do you get out of bed in the morning?

If we can establish that, and don't worry we will help you, we can both understand that reasoning and help you shout why you make what you make or do what you do to your website visitors, your customers before giving them a technical rundown of your product or service.

Step 2. Tell us your goal.

What would you like to achieve through working with us?

Tell us what you want and we can tailor our services to suit your business.

Step 3. Raise your ranking.

Search engines algorithmically read your website to learn who you are and what it is you offer their searchers. How well you deliver that content is scored for or against your website and thus affects where you rank in search results.

This step is all about improving your website to deliver your content, faster and in a more user-friendly way with all of the additional information a search engine bot will need.

Step 4. Create content people want.

Approximatly 60% of web traffic originates from a search engine so they know a huge amount about internet users. We will guide you to better position yourself and your content to help it be found and more importantly be useful. It has been proven that promoting indirectly is a more successful method to drive traffic to your website.

Through utilising the information published by the search engines and analytics we will guide you towards creating content that target keyword and phrases people actually search for.

Step 5. Maintaining your success.

It is one thing to create optimise your site and create great content once but to really rise in the rankings and keep people on your site takes perseverance.

We will create content and management stratiegies along with monthly website health check reports to monitor and maintain your improvements.
